📂 Critical Step: Allow Access to Local File URLs

If you view PDFs stored on your local drive (`file:///` URLs), Chrome requires a 1-time permission setup:

  1. Open chrome://extensions/ in a new tab.
  2. Find PDF Dark Mode and click Details.
  3. Toggle switch for "Allow access to file URLs" to ON.
